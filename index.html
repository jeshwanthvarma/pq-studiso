About PQ Studios

The story behind Hyderabad's premier photography studio

Sai Kumar Maddina

Founder & Lead Photographer at PQ Studios Hyderabad

With over a decade of professional photography experience, I founded PQ Studios with a vision to create timeless visual stories that capture the essence of every moment. Based in Hyderabad, we serve clients across India with exceptional photography services.

My journey in photography began with formal education in visual arts, complemented by years of hands-on experience working with diverse clients. This combination of academic knowledge and practical expertise allows me to approach each photography project with both artistic vision and technical precision.

At PQ Studios Hyderabad, we believe that great photography is more than just taking pictures - it's about creating visual narratives that resonate emotionally and stand the test of time. Our photography studio is equipped with state-of-the-art equipment and our team is trained in the latest photography techniques.
