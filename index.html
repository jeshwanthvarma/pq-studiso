About Us

The story behind PQ Studios

Sai Kumar Maddina

Founder & Lead Photographer

With over a decade of professional photography experience, I founded PQ Studios with a vision to create timeless visual stories that capture the essence of every moment.

My journey in photography began with formal education in visual arts, complemented by years of hands-on experience working with diverse clients. This combination of academic knowledge and practical expertise allows me to approach each project with both artistic vision and technical precision.

At PQ Studios, we believe that great photography is more than just taking pictures - it's about creating visual narratives that resonate emotionally and stand the test of time.
